With our engineering notebook, we had a couple of specific goals.

  1. Involve the whole team in writing entries
  2. Encourage anaytical thought of the progress we're making
  3. Convey our design process as clearly as possible to outside readers
When writing our entries, there are 4 basic concepts we address:
  1. A summary of what we've done- So we know the basics of what's going on.
  2. Justication for our actions- To affirm that we're making progress.
  3. Any challenges we may have faced- To then address how we came up with solutions.
  4. Reflection on how the robot is affected- To consider the effect of our actions, and make sure we don't hinder someobody else's work.

From Digital to Physical Form

Having an online EN has many benefits, including increased legibility, ease of access for members to contribue their entries, and the ability to insert video clips, GIFS, and references to outside resources. Of course, these aren't easily conveyed when our EN is in it is printed out. That's why this page is dedicated to enhacing our physical EN by providing easy access to the aspects of our EN that just can't be conveyed on paper.
